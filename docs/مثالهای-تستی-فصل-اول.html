مثال 35 آهنگ جابجایی یک مورچه \(3.6 \frac{cm}{s}\) است. این آهنگ تقریبا چند \(\frac{km}{h}\) است؟

نمایش پاسخ \[ \begin{aligned} 3.6 \frac{\overset{\overset{-2}{\uparrow}}{c}m}{s} & \underset{ \overrightarrow{s \underset{\div}{<} h} }{\overset{\underrightarrow{-2-(3)=-5}}{=}} ? \frac{\overset{\overset{-3}{\uparrow}}{k}m}{h} \\ & = 3.6 \times \cfrac{10^{-5}}{\frac{1}{3600}} \times \frac{km}{h} \\ & = \cancelto{4^{2}}{3.6^{2}} \times 10^{-2} \frac{km}{h} \\ & \cong 1.6 \times 10^{-1} \frac{km}{h} \end{aligned} \]

مثال 36 اعداد کدام گزینه به ترتیب می‌توانند به جای علامت سوال تبدیل واحد‌های زیر قرار گیرند؟

\(4dm=?\mu m, 8.2cm=?pm, 5000m=?Mm, 0.048km=?nm\)

  1. \(4 \times 10^{-5}, 8.2 \times 10^{-10}, 5 \times 10^{3}, 4.8 \times 10^{-4}\)
  2. \(4 \times 10^{-5}, 8.2 \times 10^{10}, 5 \times 10^{-3}, 4.8 \times 10^{-4}\)
  3. \(4 \times 10^{5}, 8.2 \times 10^{10}, 5 \times 10^{-3}, 4.8 \times 10^{4}\)
  4. \(4 \times 10^{5}, 8.2 \times 10^{-10}, 5 \times 10^{3}, 4.8 \times 10^{4}\)
نمایش پاسخ

گزینه ۳:

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 4 \overset{\overset{-1}{\uparrow}}{d} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}} & \overset{\underrightarrow{-1-(-6)\times1=5}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{-6}{\uparrow}}{\mu} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}} \\ 4dm & = 4 \times 10^{5} \mu m \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 8.2 \overset{\overset{-2}{\uparrow}}{c} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}} & \overset{\underrightarrow{-2-(-12)\times1=10}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{-12}{\uparrow}}{p} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}} \\ 8.2dm & = 8.2 \times 10^{10} pm \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 5\overset{\overset{3}{\uparrow}}{000} \overset{\overset{0}{\uparrow}}{} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}} & \overset{\underrightarrow{3 + (0 - 6) \times 1 = -3}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{6}{\uparrow}}{M} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}} \\ 5000m & = 5 \times 10^{-3} Mm \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} \overset{\overset{-2}{\uparrow}}{0.0}48 \overset{\overset{3}{\uparrow}}{k} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}} & \overset{\underrightarrow{-2 + (3 - (-9)) \times 1 = 10}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{-9}{\uparrow}}{n} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}} \\ 0.048 dm & = 4.8 \times 10^{10} nm \end{aligned} \]

مثال 37 کدام گزینه به ترتیب می‌توانند به جای علامت سوال تبدیل واحد‌های زیر قرار گیرند؟

\(50cm^2=? mm^2, m^2=?mm^2, 2000 \mu m^2=?m^2, 0.008m^2=?mm^2\)

  1. \(5 \times 10^{6}, 10^{6}, 2 \times 10^{-12}, 8 \times 10^{-9}\)
  2. \(5 \times 10^{3}, 10^{6}, 2 \times 10^{-9}, 8 \times 10^{9}\)
  3. \(5 \times 10^{6}, 10^{-6}, 2 \times 10^{-12}, 8 \times 10^{-9}\)
  4. \(5 \times 10^{3}, 10^{-6}, 2 \times 10^{-9}, 8 \times 10^{-9}\)
نمایش پاسخ

گزینه ۲:

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 5\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{0} \overset{\overset{-2}{\uparrow}}{c} m^2 & \overset{\underrightarrow{1+(-2 - (-3)) \times 2 = 3}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{-3}{\uparrow}}{m} m^2 \\ 50 cm^2 & = 5 \times 10^{3} mm^{2} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 1 \overset{\overset{0}{\uparrow}}{} m^2 & \overset{\underrightarrow{0 - (-3) \times 2 = 6}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{-3}{\uparrow}}{m} m^2 \\ 1 cm^2 & = 10^{6} mm^{2} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 2\overset{\overset{3}{\uparrow}}{000} \overset{\overset{-6}{\uparrow}}{\mu} m^2 & \overset{\underrightarrow{3 + (-6-0) \times 2 = -9}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{0}{\uparrow}}{} m^2 \\ 2000 \mu m^2 & = 2 \times 10^{-9} m^{2} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} \overset{\overset{-3}{\uparrow}}{0.00}8 \overset{\overset{3}{\uparrow}}{k} m^2 & \overset{\underrightarrow{-3 + (3-(-3)) \times 2 = 9}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{-3}{\uparrow}}{m} m^2 \\ 0.008 km^2 & = 8 \times 10^{9} mm^{2} \end{aligned} \]

مثال 38 کدام گزینه به ترتیب می‌توانند به جای علامت سوال تبدیل واحد‌های زیر قرار گیرند؟

\(1m^3=? cm^3, 1200 mm^3=?m^3, 40 L=?mm^3\)

  1. \(10^{-6}, 1.2 \times 10^{6}, 4 \times 10^{7}, 10^{15}\)
  2. \(10^{6}, 1.2 \times 10^{6}, 4 \times 10^{-7}, 10^{15}\)
  3. \(10^{-6}, 1.2 \times 10^{-6}, 4 \times 10^{-7}, 10^{15}\)
  4. \(10^{6}, 1.2 \times 10^{-6}, 4 \times 10^{7}, 10^{-15}\)
نمایش پاسخ

گزینه ۴:

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 1 \overset{\overset{0}{\uparrow}}{} m^3 & \overset{\underrightarrow{(0 - (-2)) \times 3 = 6}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{-2}{\uparrow}}{c} m^3 \\ 1 m^3 & = 10^{6} cm^{3} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 1\overset{\overset{3}{\uparrow}}{200} \overset{\overset{-3}{\uparrow}}{m} m^3 & \overset{\underrightarrow{3+(-3-0)\times3=-6}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{0}{\uparrow}}{} m^3 \\ 1200 mm^3 & = 1.2 \times 10^{-6} cm^{3} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 4 \overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{0} \overset{\overset{ \large \overset{\overset{-1}{\uparrow}}{d}m^3}{\uparrow} }{L} & \overset{\underrightarrow{1 + (-1-(-3)) \times 3 = 7}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{-3}{\uparrow}}{m} m^3 \\ 40 L & = 4 \times 10^{7} mm^{3} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 1 \overset{\overset{-6}{\uparrow}}{\mu} m^3 & \overset{\underrightarrow{(-6-(-1))\times3=-15}}{=} ? \overset{\overset{ \large\overset{\overset{-1}{\uparrow}}{d}m^3}{\uparrow} }{L} \\ 1 \mu m^3 & = 10^{-15} L \end{aligned} \]

مثال 39 کدام گزینه به ترتیب می‌توانند به جای علامت سوال تبدیل واحد‌های زیر قرار گیرند؟

\[ 1\frac{g}{cm^3}=?\frac{kg}{m^3}, 1\frac{kg}{m^3}=?\frac{g}{L}, 1\frac{g}{mm^3}=?\frac{kg}{L} \]

  1. \(10^{3}, 1, 5 \times 10^{4}\)
  2. \(10^{-3}, -1, 5 \times 10^{-4}\)
  3. \(10^{3}, -1, 5 \times 10^{-4}\)
  4. \(10^{-3}, 1, 5 \times 10^{-4}\)
نمایش پاسخ

گزینه ۱:

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 1 \cfrac{\overset{\overset{0}{\uparrow}}{} g }{\underset{\underset{-2}{\downarrow}}{c}m^3} & \overset{\underrightarrow{(0-3) - (-2-0) \times 3 = 3}}{=} ? \cfrac{\overset{\overset{3}{\uparrow}}{k} g }{\underset{\underset{0}{\downarrow}}{}m^3} \\ 1\frac{g}{cm^3} & = 10^{3} \frac{kg}{m^3} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 1 \cfrac{\overset{\overset{3}{\uparrow}}{k} g }{\underset{\underset{0}{\downarrow}}{} m^3} & \overset{\underrightarrow{(3-0) - (0-(-1)) \times 3 = 1}}{=} ? \cfrac{\overset{\overset{0}{\uparrow}}{} g }{\underset{\underset{ \large\underset{\underset{-1}{\downarrow}}{d}m^3}{\downarrow} }{L}} \\ 1\frac{kg}{m^3} & = 1 \frac{g}{L} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 5 \overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{0} \cfrac{\overset{\overset{0}{\uparrow}}{} g }{\underset{\underset{-3}{\downarrow}}{m}m^3} & \overset{\underrightarrow{1 + (0-3) - (-3-(-1)) \times 3 = 4}}{=} ? \cfrac{\overset{\overset{3}{\uparrow}}{k} g }{\underset{\underset{ \large\underset{\underset{-1}{\downarrow}}{d}m^3}{\downarrow} }{L}} \\ 1\frac{g}{mm^3} & = 5 \times 10^{4} \frac{kg}{L} \end{aligned} \]

مثال 40 کدام گزینه به ترتیب می‌توانند به جای علامت سوال تبدیل واحد‌های زیر قرار گیرند؟

\[ 1\frac{km}{h}=?\frac{m}{s}, 1\frac{cm}{s}=?\frac{mm}{min} \]

  1. \(\cfrac{10}{3.6}, 0.6\)
  2. \(\cfrac{1}{36}, 6\)
  3. \(\cfrac{1}{3.6}, 60\)
  4. \(\cfrac{1}{3.6}, 600\)
نمایش پاسخ

گزینه ۴:

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 1 \frac{ \overset{\overset{3}{\uparrow}}{k} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}} }{h} & \underset{ \overrightarrow{h \underset{\times}{>}min \underset{\times}{>}s} }{\overset{\underrightarrow{(3-0) \times 1 = 3}}{=}} ? \frac{\overset{\overset{0}{\uparrow}}{} m^{\overset{\overset{1}{\uparrow}}{}}}{s} \\ & = \frac{10^{3}}{1 \times 60 \times 60} \times \cfrac{m}{s} \\ & = \cfrac{1}{3.6} \frac{m}{s} \end{aligned} \]

\[ \require{cancel} \begin{aligned} 1 \frac{\overset{\overset{-2}{\uparrow}}{c}m}{s} & \underset{ \overrightarrow{s \underset{\div}{<} min} }{\overset{\underrightarrow{-2-(-3) = 1}}{=}} ? \frac{\overset{\overset{-3}{\uparrow}}{m}m}{min} \\ & = \frac{10}{\frac{1}{60}} \times \cfrac{mm}{min} \\ & = 600 \frac{mm}{min} \end{aligned} \]
